Save smarter with transparent financial coaching for Sierra Leone.

SavWise AI is an open-source Digital Public Good prototype that helps users track money, set savings goals, detect risky spending, and ask an AI coach for educational guidance.

Enter Demo AppView Features

Prototype only: no real Orange Money or Afrimoney account is connected, and the system does not transfer money.

MVP demonstration flow

  1. Accept the privacy notice
  2. Add income and expense transactions
  3. View your financial health score
  4. Create savings goals and track progress
  5. Check if a purchase is affordable
  6. Ask Wizz for educational financial guidance
  7. Export JSON or CSV or reset data

Dynamic dashboard

See your income, spending, health score, alerts, and emergency safety.

Wizz Financial Coach

Get educational budgeting and savings guidance based on your data. Powered by Groq through the backend.

Privacy-first design

Includes consent, privacy pages, MIT licence, JSON/CSV export and deletion controls.

Open-source and privacy-by-design

This MVP is built for DLAW207 I.T Law and IPR Legal Issues. It demonstrates responsible data handling, interoperability, explainable financial rules and an open-source structure suitable for a Digital Public Good.
