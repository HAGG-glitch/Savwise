# Ethical AI

The financial score, risk alerts, goal prediction, and affordability checker use transparent rules. The Groq coach is used only for educational financial-literacy guidance.

The AI prompt instructs the model not to claim to be a bank, lender, lawyer, investment adviser, or licensed professional. It must not ask for PINs, OTPs, bank passwords, national ID numbers, or confidential KYC information.

Future AI work should include bias review, safety testing, human oversight, consented data use, and monitoring.
